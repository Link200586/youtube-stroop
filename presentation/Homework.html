Lets see if political affiliation and minimum wage support are independent before the participants watched Americas Funniest Home Videos. To do this I need to calculate the frequency that individuals appear in different political groups. Then Ill use a Chi-Square test of independence to test the hypothesis that affiliation and support are independent. Again, I dont want to count people twice, so Ill focus on the posttest data.

tab<-table(pols$party[pols$testtime=="pre"],pols$minwage[pols$testtime=="pre"])
